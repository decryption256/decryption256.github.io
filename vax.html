Answers to Questions People Ask

What a great offer! How do I get a COVID-19 vaccination?
It can be confusing to find locations offering the COVID-19 vaccine. The two best sources are the federal government's vaccine eligibility checker and the very handy COVID-19 Near Me. Many chemist chains (e.g: Priceline, Amcal, Terry White) are offering COVID-19 vaccinations, as are GPs which can be booked online via HotDoc and HealthEngine.

Are you allowed to encourage people to get vaccinated?
Yes, the TGA's website has information specific for businesses offering rewards to people who get COVID-19 vaccinations.

This help document from Services Australia outlines how to get proof of your COVID-19 vaccinations.
